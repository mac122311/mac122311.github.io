About Us

Owner and Story: I am a veteran owned establishment that is also black owned. I am trying to bring fun to the dining experience to bring fantasies of all ages together. We have been in business for six years and I enjoy everything about it. I love to cater to each customer and make sure they have the greatest time. the food is always made to order and a la cart.
